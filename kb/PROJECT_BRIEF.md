# iGift — Project Brief

## One-Liner
Trust-scored deal intelligence for digital gift cards, credits, and vouchers.

## Problem
Gift cards, prepaid credits, vouchers, and similar digital-value products are sold across many websites, but buyers have no reliable way to know which offers are truly good, usable, and safe. Existing options are fragmented across authorized retailers, secondary marketplaces, and deal communities — with inconsistent fee transparency, weak region-compatibility information, and mixed trust signals.

## Solution
A deal-intelligence layer that discovers, verifies, and ranks lawful digital-value deals across sources, normalizes them across regions and currencies, and helps users separate real opportunities from misleading, risky, or low-quality listings.

## What We Are
- A deal discovery and verification platform
- A publisher and affiliate referral site
- A premium alerts service
- Later: a B2B data/API provider

## What We Are NOT
- A marketplace or store
- A payment processor or escrow
- A broker or reseller of stored value
- A scraping-as-a-service business

## Target Users
1. **Deal hunters** — consumers looking for discounted gift cards and digital credits
2. **Gamers** — buying Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo credits at a discount
3. **Gift givers** — looking for the best price on gift cards they plan to give
4. **Power users** — professionals or enthusiasts who regularly buy digital value products
5. **B2B buyers** (later) — corporate procurement, employee perks platforms, savings apps

## Business Model (Phased)
1. Affiliate click-outs (primary V1 revenue)
2. Premium watchlists / price alerts (subscription)
3. Team/pro subscription tier
4. Sponsored placements with strict ad labeling
5. B2B data/API licensing

## MVP Scope (V1)
- 10-15 sources (APIs, feeds, public pages)
- 3-5 categories (gaming, app stores, streaming, retail, food)
- US + Global region support
- Daily/hourly refresh
- Email alerts
- Manual moderation for new sources

## Excluded from V1
- Account resale / credential listings
- Open-loop prepaid cards
- Direct checkout / wallet / escrow
- User-to-user marketplace
- Full autonomous web scraping
- Multi-language frontend

## Success Metrics
- Valid-offer rate above 90-92%
- Outbound CTR above 4%
- Repeat usage via alerts/watchlists
- Low complaint/takedown rate

## Kill Criteria
- Invalid/unusable offers stay above 15%
- Merchant complaints or takedowns are frequent
- Affiliate economics are too weak
- Users don't care about verification enough to return
